Clicker

Description:

This is a game, made for a school project in Apache Netbeans, with programming language java. There are 3 different game modes: speed (you need to click the circle as fast as you can, but that circle moves every time you click), accuracy (same as before, but there will be 3 boxes, different sizes and the most centered box gives you the most points, the outer box gives you the least) and reaction (click as fast as you can when the screen turns red).
